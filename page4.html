1) The Buckle

The metal buckle of the Belt is produced abroad by a
subsidiary of the commercial vehicle company ACT Research.

After being cut from a metal sheet, the parts are carefully folded into our buckle.
During finishing the buckles are meticulously sanded before they receive their final colour.
The use of powder coating and high quality finish guarantees the buckles remain stainless and keep their colour.
De metalen buckle van de Belt-riem wordt geproduceerd in het buitenland door een dochteronderneming van de autogarage ACT Research.

ACT Research

2) Cutting to size

After recycling seatbelts from car wrecks all over the country we
cut them to size both in length and in width. 

Wemar Cars

3) Finishing the belt 

Having cut the belts to size, we singe the edges to prevent fraying. We then stitch two
belts of equal length together to construct our basic belt and finish the edges with
banding. As a final step we attach a belt clasp to the free end of the belt.
