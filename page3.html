Wearing Belt is to Buckle Up in the car. Our enterprise supports the charity SAVE (parents of child road victims). SAVE offers help, advice and support to families of young fatal road victims. By buying one of our belts you also support SAVE’s campaigns for increased traffic safety. For each belt sold we donate €1 to this project.
